The Garden City & Silicon Valley of India

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is India's tech capital and one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the country. Known for its pleasant weather, lush gardens, vibrant pub culture, and booming IT industry, Bangalore offers a perfect blend of modernity and tradition. From historic palaces to cutting-edge tech parks, from peaceful parks to buzzing nightlife, the city has something for everyone. Whether you're a foodie, nature lover, history enthusiast, or party-goer, Bangalore welcomes you with open arms!

  • Talakadu (135 km) - Buried temples in sand dunes. Unique archaeological site on the Kaveri river.

üí° Essential Bangalore Travel Tips

Navigate Bangalore Like a Local

October to February for pleasant weather. Bangalore has great weather year-round, but avoid peak summer (March-May).

Use Namma Metro, Uber/Ola cabs, or auto-rickshaws. Traffic can be heavy, so plan accordingly. Metro is fastest during rush hours.

Koramangala and Indiranagar for nightlife, MG Road for shopping, Whitefield near IT parks. Options from budget to luxury available.

Generally safe city, but stick to main areas at night. Use registered cabs for late-night travel. Pubs close by 1 AM.
